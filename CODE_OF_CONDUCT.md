# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and respectful environment for everyone,
regardless of background or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus on technical issues, not people.
- Accept feedback and correct mistakes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or pull requests
that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately via the security contact in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.
